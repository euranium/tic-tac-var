How to play

There are 16 pieces with four different characteristics: X/Y, captital/lower case, italics/regular, black/yellow. Each player takes turns selecting a piece for the oponent who then places it on the board. The first player to possition a line of four characters with a similar characteristic wins. You do not have to be the one to lay the other three pieces down.
